How to Evaluate a SaaS Business Before Acquisition

Complete due diligence framework for SaaS acquisition targets.

Acquiring a SaaS business without proper due diligence is like buying a house without an inspection. Revenue can look healthy on the surface while hiding serious retention problems.

The 5-Point SaaS Due Diligence Framework

  • Revenue quality: is MRR sustainable or inflated by one-time deals?
  • Cohort retention: are older cohorts retaining or churning over time?
  • Customer concentration: does one customer represent more than 10% of MRR?
  • Churn velocity: is churn accelerating or decelerating?
  • Expansion potential: is there room for upsell and cross-sell?

Red Flags in SaaS Acquisition

Watch for: declining NRR, increasing CAC, shrinking expansion revenue, customer concentration risk, and churn that spikes after annual contract renewals.
