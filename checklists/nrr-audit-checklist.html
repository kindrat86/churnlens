NRR Audit Checklist

A checklist for auditing the accuracy and completeness of your Net Revenue Retention calculation.

How to use: work through each section in order. Don't skip steps — the order matters.

Inputs

  • Starting MRR by cohort correctly captured
  • Expansion MRR properly attributed
  • Contraction MRR properly attributed
  • Churn MRR properly attributed

Classification

  • Annual vs monthly correctly tagged
  • Currency normalization applied
  • Proration rules consistent
  • Trial-to-paid conversions captured

Audit trail

  • Every movement traceable to an account
  • Reconciliation against billing system
  • Historical restatements documented
  • Cohort definitions stable over time

Why this checklist exists

This checklist was built for teams handling churn due diligence tool. Each item addresses a common failure mode we've seen in real workflows. Skipping a step doesn't save time — it creates rework, disputes, or missed signals downstream.
