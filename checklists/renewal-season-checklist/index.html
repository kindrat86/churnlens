SaaS Renewal Season Checklist

A pre-renewal-season checklist to maximize retention and surface at-risk accounts early.

How to use: work through each section in order. Don't skip steps — the order matters.

90 days out

  • Top 50 accounts identified
  • Health scores current
  • Expansion opportunities mapped
  • CSM assignments confirmed

60 days out

  • Outreach to all at-risk accounts
  • Save offers prepared
  • Exec sponsors engaged on top accounts
  • Contract changes flagged

30 days out

  • Renewal confirmations tracked
  • Auto-renew vs manual-renew status
  • Payment methods verified
  • Backup plans for likely churns

Why this checklist exists

This checklist was built for teams handling churn due diligence tool. Each item addresses a common failure mode we've seen in real workflows. Skipping a step doesn't save time — it creates rework, disputes, or missed signals downstream.
