Healthtech SaaS Churn Analysis

Healthtech SaaS carries churn patterns invisible to standard analytics. HIPAA-related pauses, hospital credentialing delays, and multi-year enterprise contracts mask true churn in subscription data. Churn Lens surfaces the real retention story — flagging accounts paused for compliance review that would spike churn on renewal, modeling concentration when a 3-hospital system represents 50% of MRR, and separating credentialing-delay churn from product dissatisfaction.

Key healthtech churn signals

  • HIPAA compliance pauses inflating retention metrics
  • Hospital system concentration (3 hospitals = 50% MRR)
  • Credentialing delays masking true churn risk
  • Compliance-flag vs voluntary churn separation
