AI SaaS companies have unique retention patterns — usage-based pricing churn, token cost sensitivity, and model-switching retention data.

AI SaaS companies face churn drivers that don't exist in traditional SaaS: usage-based pricing churn (customers leaving when token costs surprise them), model-switching retention (customers who move to cheaper models), and free-tier cannibalization. These benchmarks help you evaluate an AI SaaS acquisition against its true peers — not generic SaaS metrics.
