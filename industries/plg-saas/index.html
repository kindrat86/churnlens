Product-Led Growth SaaS Churn Benchmarks

PLG SaaS companies have unique retention patterns — free-to-paid conversion rates, product-qualified churn, and expansion revenue benchmarks.

Product-led growth SaaS companies convert users through product experience, not sales calls. This creates unique churn patterns: free-to-paid conversion churn (users who try and leave), product-qualified churn (users who never reach the activation milestone), and expansion revenue from self-serve upgrades. These benchmarks help acquirers evaluate whether a PLG company's retention is healthy or hiding structural churn.
