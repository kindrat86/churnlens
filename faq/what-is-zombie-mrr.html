What Is Zombie MRR in SaaS?

Zombie MRR is subscription revenue from customers who are paying — every month — but have effectively abandoned the product. They haven't logged in. They haven't opened a support ticket. They're paying out of inertia, forgetfulness, or because cancellation is too hard. For SaaS acquirers, zombie MRR is dangerous because it inflates both MRR and implied retention rates. When those zombies finally cancel — and they will — the churn rate spikes and the buyer absorbs the decay.
