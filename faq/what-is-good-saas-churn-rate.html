What Is a Good SaaS Churn Rate?

Good churn rates depend on your customer segment: enterprise SaaS should have <1% monthly churn, mid-market 1-3%, SMB 3-5%, and B2C SaaS 5-7%. But these benchmarks assume honest reporting — sellers often exclude annual-plan customers, zombie MRR, and paused accounts from their churn calculations. Always validate with raw subscription data.
