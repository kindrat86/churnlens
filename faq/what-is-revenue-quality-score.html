What Is a SaaS Revenue Quality Score?

A Revenue Quality Score (RQS) is a 0-100 composite metric that rates SaaS subscription revenue on five dimensions: retention stability (is monthly churn consistent?), concentration risk (can one cancellation sink the business?), expansion efficiency (is NRR growth real or zombie upgrades?), payment hygiene (how much MRR is delinquent?), and contract durability (annual vs monthly mix). RQS was built for SaaS acquirers who need to look past headline MRR to the real durability story.
