What Is Revenue Concentration Risk?

Revenue concentration risk is the danger that a single customer's cancellation wipes out your returns. A SaaS with 3% monthly churn looks fine — until you learn one customer is 40% of MRR. Concentration risk is measured with the Herfindahl-Hirschman Index (HHI): calculate each customer's share of total MRR, square it, sum them. Scores above 0.25 are dangerous; above 0.40 are deal-breakers without structural protections.
