What Is the SaaS Renewal Cliff?

The SaaS renewal cliff happens when a large cohort of annual-plan customers all reach their renewal date simultaneously. Before renewal, monthly churn looks artificially low — these customers can't leave because they're locked in. At renewal, they all decide at once, and the real churn rate reveals itself overnight. For acquirers, detecting the renewal cliff before buying is critical: ask the seller for an annual-plan renewal schedule, not just a churn percentage.
