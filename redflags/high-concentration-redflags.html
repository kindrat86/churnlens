Revenue Concentration Red Flags

Concentration patterns that signal a SaaS target's revenue is fragile and churn-exposed.

How to use: scan the list against your current situation. Each item you check is a risk worth addressing before it becomes a problem.
  • Top customer exceeds 10% of ARR
  • Top 10 customers exceed 40% of ARR
  • Single industry exceeds 50% of revenue
  • Single geographic market exceeds 60%
  • Founder-led relationships with top accounts
  • No customer success coverage on top 10

What to do if you see these

If more than 2-3 of these flags apply to your churn due diligence tool situation, treat it as a structural issue, not a one-off. The compounding effect of multiple red flags is greater than the sum of the individual risks.
