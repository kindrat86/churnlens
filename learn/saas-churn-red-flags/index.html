SaaS Churn Red Flags

Ten churn red flags every SaaS acquirer must check before signing: revenue concentration, logo churn acceleration, expansion stall, and hidden annual plan risk.

Before you acquire a SaaS business, check these 10 churn red flags: (1) Revenue concentration — does one customer represent >15% of MRR? (2) Logo churn acceleration — is customer loss speeding up quarter-over-quarter? (3) Expansion revenue stall — are existing customers no longer upgrading? (4) Hidden annual plan risk — how many annual contracts renew next quarter? (5) Support ticket correlation — do support spikes predict churn 30 days later? (6-10: product usage decline, discount dependency, platform risk, founder dependency, market saturation.)
