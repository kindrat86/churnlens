What the benchmarks show

Median net revenue retention for private B2B SaaS sits around 101–102% (Benchmarkit 2025; KeyBanc 2024). It climbs steadily with contract value — bigger deals expand more and churn less.

Annual contract value (ACV)Median net revenue retention
Under $12K100%
$12K–$25K102%
$25K–$50K103%
$50K–$100K105%
$100K–$250K107%
Over $250K110%

Source: SaaS Capital, 2023 B2B SaaS Retention Benchmarks; overall median corroborated by Benchmarkit (101%, FY2024) and KeyBanc/Sapphire (~101%).

Top quartile: enterprise vendors with ACV above $100K report NRR of 118–120% at the top quartile. Gross vs net: median gross revenue retention is ~88–91%, so the gap up to NRR is expansion revenue.

These figures are curated from public SaaS benchmarking research — including SaaS Capital, Benchmarkit, Recurly, High Alpha, and First Page Sage — and organized for buyer-side due diligence.
