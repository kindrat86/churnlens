What the benchmarks show

Expansion is now the largest single source of new ARR for many SaaS companies. In Benchmarkit’s 2025 report (FY2024 data), the median company generated 40% of total new ARR from expansion — up 5 points year over year — at a median expansion CAC of $1.00 to add $1 of expansion ARR, versus $2.00 for new-customer ARR.
