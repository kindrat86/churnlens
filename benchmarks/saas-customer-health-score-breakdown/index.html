SaaS Customer Health Score Components

Common components of a SaaS customer health score — weightings vary by company, not an industry standard

Customer health scores typically combine product usage and adoption, support activity, NPS/sentiment, expansion signals, and login engagement. There is no single industry-standard formula — weightings are configured per company and product. Any specific weighting (for example a usage-heavy split) is illustrative, not a published benchmark.
