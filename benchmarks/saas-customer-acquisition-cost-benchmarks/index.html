What the benchmarks show

Median CAC payback for private B2B SaaS is 18 months (Benchmarkit 2025, FY2024) — up from 16 in 2022 — with the middle 50% of companies between 12 and 24 months. Payback lengthens sharply as deal size grows.

CAC ratio: a median $2.00 of sales & marketing wins $1 of new-customer ARR (expansion is far cheaper at $1.00). LTV:CAC: segment medians run ~3–4:1 for scaled companies and compress above $20M ARR; First Page Sage cites a 4:1 benchmark — First Page Sage.

These figures are curated from public SaaS benchmarking research — including SaaS Capital, Benchmarkit, Recurly, High Alpha, and First Page Sage — and organized for buyer-side due diligence.
