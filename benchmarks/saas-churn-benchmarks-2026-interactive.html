How to Use These Benchmarks

1. Find your vertical — filter by segment above.

2. Compare both metrics — logo churn and revenue churn tell different stories. A company with 1% logo churn but 5% revenue churn has a concentration problem.

3. Check your NRR — if your Net Revenue Retention is below 100%, expansion revenue isn't offsetting churn. This is a red flag for acquirers.

4. Annual plan percentage — companies with >70% annual plans have artificially low monthly churn. The renewal cliff is coming.

Methodology

Dataset aggregated from public SaaS company filings (10-K, S-1), M&A marketplace listings (Acquire.com, MicroAcquire, Firstbase), ChurnLens user-contributed anonymous data, and industry surveys. Companies with <50K ARR excluded. Self-reported churn validated against raw subscription data where available. Last updated: July 2026.
