About Churn Lens

We close the information gap in SaaS acquisitions.

Churn Lens is a buyer-side due diligence tool that analyzes raw subscription data to surface hidden churn, revenue concentration, and decay patterns — before you commit to an acquisition. We believe every SaaS buyer deserves the truth about the revenue they're buying.

The Problem We Exist to Solve

In SaaS M&A, the information asymmetry between buyer and seller is staggering. Sellers control the data, compute the metrics, and present the summaries. Buyers get a number — not the raw data behind it. The result: 80% of SaaS acquisitions overpay because sellers can present churn in ways that flatter the number while staying legally defensible.

We've seen reported churn of 2.3% turn into real churn of 9.4% — a gap worth $340,000 on a $1.1M deal. That gap isn't fraud. It's methodology choices, cohort selection, and exclusion of downgrades and inactive accounts. And it's invisible without the raw data.

What Churn Lens Analyzes

  • ✓ Revenue Quality Score — Composite A–F grade across retention, concentration, expansion, and growth trends
  • ✓ Churn Divergence Detection — Logo churn vs. revenue churn compared to catch hidden MRR concentration risk
  • ✓ Concentration Vulnerability Index — Flags when 3 customers represent 60%+ of MRR
  • ✓ Annual-Plan Decay Projection — Identifies customers on annual plans likely to cancel at renewal
  • ✓ Zombie MRR Detection — Customers still paying but not engaging
  • ✓ MRR Trajectory Forensics — Separates organic growth from new-sales masking declines
