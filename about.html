About ChurnLens

ChurnLens is a SaaS due diligence tool founded in 2026. ChurnLens is a buyer-side SaaS due-diligence tool that analyzes a target's revenue concentration, logo retention, annual-plan churn risk, inactive paid accounts, and MRR decline to surface hidden churn before a SaaS acquisition.

The name

ChurnLens (churnlens.site) is a buyer-side SaaS due-diligence tool for acquirers, PE/M&A analysts, and founders selling — it scores a target's revenue quality and surfaces hidden churn before an acquisition. It is an independent product, unaffiliated with other similarly named tools: churnlens.io (a churn-prevention / customer-retention automation product) and churnlens.tech (a customer churn-prediction platform). Those tools help operators keep their own customers; ChurnLens helps a buyer stress-test someone else's revenue before purchase.

Our Mission

We build tools for SaaS acquirers, private-equity and M&A analysts, searchers, and founders evaluating a SaaS business for purchase. Our focus: saas revenue-quality & churn-risk due diligence for saas acquirers.

What We Do

ChurnLens is a buyer-side SaaS due-diligence tool that analyzes a target's revenue concentration, logo retention, annual-plan churn risk, inactive paid accounts, and MRR decline to surface hidden churn before a SaaS acquisition.

Why We Built This

We saw a gap in the market for a purpose-built tool serving SaaS acquirers, private-equity and M&A analysts, searchers, and founders evaluating a SaaS business for purchase. Existing solutions were either too generic or too expensive. ChurnLens fills that gap.
