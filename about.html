About ChurnLens

ChurnLens is a SaaS due diligence tool founded in 2026. ChurnLens analyzes revenue concentration, logo retention, annual plan churn risk, and MRR decline patterns to surface hidden churn before a SaaS acquisition.

Our Mission

We build tools for SaaS acquirers, PE analysts, founders evaluating SaaS businesses for purchase. Our focus: saas revenue quality & churn risk due diligence.

What We Do

ChurnLens analyzes revenue concentration, logo retention, annual plan churn risk, and MRR decline patterns to surface hidden churn before a SaaS acquisition.

Why We Built This

We saw a gap in the market for a purpose-built tool serving SaaS acquirers, PE analysts, founders evaluating SaaS businesses for purchase. Existing solutions were either too generic or too expensive. ChurnLens fills that gap.
