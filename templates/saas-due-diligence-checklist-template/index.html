SaaS Due Diligence Checklist Template

A comprehensive due diligence checklist template for acquiring a SaaS business, covering revenue quality, churn, and retention.

Format: Markdown / PDF · Free to use · Adapted for churn due diligence tool workflows

What's included

Revenue quality

  • MRR/ARR breakdown by cohort
  • Revenue concentration (top 5, 10, 25 customers)
  • Annual vs monthly mix
  • Expansion vs new vs contraction split

Churn analysis

  • Gross revenue churn (trailing 12mo)
  • Logo churn by segment
  • Cohort retention curves
  • Annual-plan renewal risk (next 90 days)

Customer health

  • Zombie MRR detection (inactive >60 days)
  • Product engagement by account
  • Support ticket trends
  • NPS and CSAT scores

Contract review

  • Auto-renewal clauses
  • Cancellation notice periods
  • Pricing lock-ins and ramps
  • Discount concentration
