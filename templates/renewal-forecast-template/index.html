SaaS Renewal Forecast Template

A template for forecasting upcoming SaaS renewals and identifying at-risk accounts.

Format: Markdown / PDF · Free to use · Adapted for churn due diligence tool workflows

What's included

Time horizons

  • Next 30 days: contractual renewals
  • Next 90 days: likely renewals
  • Next 12 months: full pipeline

Per-account fields

  • Customer name and ARR
  • Renewal date
  • Health score
  • Owner
  • Risk level

Roll-up

  • Total ARR up for renewal by quarter
  • At-risk ARR by quarter
  • Expected churn $ by quarter

Actions

  • Save plays for at-risk accounts
  • Expansion opportunities
  • Owner assignments
