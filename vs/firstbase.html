Churn Lens vs Firstbase

Firstbase is a deal-flow platform — helping acquirers find SaaS businesses to buy, compare listings, and manage the acquisition process. Churn Lens is the due-diligence layer: once you've found a target on Firstbase, run the subscription CSV through Churn Lens to validate the seller's churn claims before you close.

Key differences

  • Firstbase: SaaS acquisition marketplace and deal flow
  • Churn Lens: buyer-side churn forensics for due diligence
  • Firstbase helps you find deals; Churn Lens helps you validate them
  • Use both: source on Firstbase, diligence with Churn Lens
