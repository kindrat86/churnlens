ChurnLens vs Firstbase

Firstbase is a deal-flow platform — helping acquirers find SaaS businesses to buy, compare listings, and manage the acquisition process. ChurnLens is the due-diligence layer: once you've found a target on Firstbase, run the subscription CSV through ChurnLens to validate the seller's churn claims before you close.
