Churn Lens vs Paddle

Paddle is a payment and revenue platform — it tracks MRR, subscriptions, and revenue analytics for operating businesses. Churn Lens is a buyer-side due diligence tool — it analyzes seller subscription data to find hidden churn, concentration risk, and revenue decay before acquisition. They serve different stages: Paddle helps you run the business; Churn Lens helps you buy one.

Key differences

  • Paddle: merchant-of-record + revenue ops for operators
  • Churn Lens: buyer-side churn forensics for acquirers
  • Paddle tracks current-state revenue; Churn Lens models post-close decay
  • Churn Lens scores revenue quality on 5 dimensions Paddle doesn't measure
