Frequently Asked Questions

What is revenue quality scoring?

Revenue quality scoring analyzes MRR data, logo retention, and concentration metrics to produce a 0-100 score indicating the health of a SaaS company's revenue.

How does Churn Lens detect hidden churn?

Churn Lens identifies patterns in MRR decline, annual plan renewal rates, inactive paid accounts, and revenue concentration that indicate churn risk not visible in headline metrics.

What data do I need to use Churn Lens?

You need MRR data by customer, logo retention history, and plan information. The tool analyzes this data to surface acquisition risks.

Is Churn Lens only for acquisitions?

While built for due diligence, Churn Lens is also valuable for ongoing revenue health monitoring and board reporting.

How accurate is the revenue quality score?

The score is based on proven SaaS metrics frameworks. It cross-references multiple data points to minimize false positives and negatives.
